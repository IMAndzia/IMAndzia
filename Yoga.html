Why Yoga & Flexibility Matter

Yoga helps you move better and feel better. It's great for your body and your mind. Stretching your muscles makes you more flexible, reduces tightness, and helps your body stay strong and mobile.

Popular Yoga Exercises (How-To)

  • Downward Dog: Start on hands and knees. Lift hips to form a V. Keep legs straight. Stretch and breathe.
  • Child‚Äôs Pose: Kneel, sit back on your heels, stretch arms forward, forehead on the floor. Relax.
  • Cat-Cow Stretch: On hands and knees. Inhale, arch back (cow). Exhale, round spine (cat). Repeat.
  • Warrior II: Step one foot forward, bend the front knee. Stretch arms out and look forward.
  • Cobra Pose: Lie on your stomach, hands under shoulders. Push up your chest. Keep hips down.
  • Seated Forward Fold: Sit with legs straight. Reach forward toward toes. Breathe and hold.
  • Bridge Pose: Lie on your back, knees bent. Lift hips up. Squeeze glutes and breathe deeply.
