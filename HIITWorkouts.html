Why Choose HIIT?

HIIT combines short bursts of intense activity with brief recovery periods. It’s perfect for those who want results fast, with workouts that fit into any busy schedule. HIIT improves cardiovascular health, burns fat, and builds lean muscle in less time than traditional workouts.

20-Minute Full Body HIIT

4 rounds of 45 seconds work / 15 seconds rest:

  • Jump Squats
  • Push-Ups
  • Mountain Climbers
  • Burpees
  • High Knees

Tabata Torch

20 seconds on / 10 seconds off - 8 rounds per movement:

  • Kettlebell Swings
  • Box Jumps
  • Plank to Push-Up
  • Jump Lunges

Bodyweight Blitz

No equipment needed - go hard anywhere, anytime:

  • Squat Pulses
  • Fast Feet
  • Spider-Man Planks
  • Jumping Jacks

HIIT & Core Combo

Blast your abs while torching calories:

  • Mountain Climbers
  • Bicycle Crunches
  • Side Plank Dips
  • V-Ups

Remember: Always warm up before and cool down after your HIIT sessions. And don’t forget to hydrate!
